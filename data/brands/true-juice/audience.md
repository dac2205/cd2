### Target Audience: The "Burnout" Leader

**Demographics:**
- **Gender**: Female
- **Role**: Boss / Leader / Executive
- **Age**: 35-45
- **Income**: High
- **Location**: Hanoi

**Psychographics:**
- **Busy & Stressed**: Consumed by work, leading a company or large team.
- **Health Anxiety**: Has existing pathology (red flags in medical tests) or deep anxiety about health collapsing.
- **Mental State**: "Sợ nhưng bận" (Fearful but too busy to act properly).
- **Core Fear**: "Đổ gục" (Collapsing) in the middle of work/responsibilities.
- **Dependency**: Currently dependent on medicine or supplements but feels sustainable.

### Current Solutions (Ineffective)
- **Medical**: Visiting doctors, taking prescription medication (reactive).
- **Supplements**: Relying on functional foods (pills, powders).
- **Willpower**: Trying to "consciously" arrange rest and healthy eating but failing due to schedule.
