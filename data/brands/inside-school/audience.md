### The "Stuck" Expert
- **Characteristics**: Has deep expertise but struggles to communicate it simply.
- **Pain Points**: Posts getting low engagement, feeling misunderstood.
- **Goals**: Translate technical knowledge into engaging stories.

### The "Burnout" Creator
- **Characteristics**: Posting daily but seeing diminishing returns.
- **Pain Points**: Exhausted by the algorithm, running out of ideas.
- **Goals**: Create a sustainable system (Content Factory).

### The "Silent" Founder
- **Characteristics**: Building a great product but has zero online presence.
- **Pain Points**: Relying 100% on ads or referrals.
- **Goals**: Build an organic inbound channel.
